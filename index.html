Newport Robotics Group

Our Mission

The Newport Robotics Group (NRG) 948 is FIRST Robotics team based in Newport High School in Bellevue, Washington. Our mission is to teach students skills essential in their future careers. In particular, we have cultivated an inclusive environment that encourages students to lead and collaborate through our shared passion for robots. From ten students huddled in a garage to over 100 members in our own facility, we continue to unite our members, local teams, and our Bellevue community through STEM.

Sponsors

We want to thank our all of sponsors for their continued support of the Newport Robotics Group. Their commitment to this team has enabled students to engage in real world problem solving and learn how to cooperate as they explore science, technology, engineering, and mathematics firsthand. NRG would not be such a thriving and successful community without the generosity our sponsors.
